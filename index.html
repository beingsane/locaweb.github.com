Locaweb e Opensource

Nosso time é composto pelas pessoas mais talentosas e criativas do Brasil. Pessoas que constantemente estão corrigindo, discutindo e testando software para devolver a comunidade nossa experiência em sistemas de grande escala. Nós devolvemos para a comunidade, colegas e clientes o que fazemos aqui. Um dos nossos valores é que 'Fazemos mais com menos' - neste caso que nos orgulhamos do esforço e da criatividade que colocamos em todas as tarefas que temos, sem desperdício.

Além de contribuir com código, suportamos projetos que usamos através de contribuições, com tempo pago dos nossos desenvolvedores e com nosso feedback. Com isto em mente, nosso time decidiu publicar o máximo que puder neste repositório do github. Estes são software que utilizamos, que fizemos com cuidado e gosto por sistemas de larga escala. Sinta-se a vontade para fazer um fork, enviar um pull request (não esqueça de adicionar seu nome como contribuidor). E se você gosta do tipo de projetos que fazemos e sente que pode leva-los ao próximo nível, nos avise (e envie um curriculum).



Locaweb and Opensource

Our team has the most talented and creative people around Brazil. People that constantly are patching, discussing, testing and giving back to the community what we use daily on our large scale systems. It is only natural that we contribute back to the community, colleagues and customers what we do here. One of our core values is "We do more with less" - meaning in this case that we strive to put our creative power on every task that we do.

Beyond contributing with code, we support projects that we use thru contributions, paid developer time and by using and giving feedback. With that in mind, our team decided to publish the most of what we can here, at github. This is really software that we use, and crafted with all care. What you are going to find here are distinct software ranging from network controllers to data aggregation tools, hypervisor controlling to client examples in many languages and interesting experiments.

Feel free to fork, send pull requests (dont forget to add your name as contributor). And if you are interested in this kind of projects and feel that you could to take them to the next level, let us know (and send a resumé !)



  • simplestack - our real down to earth hypervisor control layer. no frills, just what is needed to scale up and out on our provisioning system.
  • quantum - quantum is part of openstack and netstack, it's a virtual network service. We've contributed with plugins, blueprints, firewall controllers and activelly put paid development time on it.
  • leela - our top notch data aggregation layer. GBs of data are stored on it, collected automatically from thousands of servers.
  • logix - our syslog to graylog2 bus - really, no system could help up to our log stream data. On some systems we have way more than 500GB/day so a different approach was needed
  • bricklayer - stop suffering the pains of build/package/repository upload right now. we constantly pass our systems thru it to be able to deploy packaged applications neatly. More than 2 years in steady production.
  • otto - our object storage experiment. needs care but it's in limited internal production. help us :)
  • locaweb-gateway-* - our payment gateway client to a lot of languages/frameworks. can't get easier than that
  • xenapi-rubyi - the name says itself, integrate your ruby chops with Xen
